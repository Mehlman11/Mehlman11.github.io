Graffiti
Booklet

This booklet was created for my Typography course. The booklet was centered around the history of graffiti letters throughout time and the evolution. I chose to do the history of graffiti letters, because I have always been interested in this style of lettering as it is prominent where I am from in Saginaw, Michigan. All of the graffiti shots were done by me and were taken in either Saginaw or Mount Pleasant, Michigan. The typeface included funky letters to stay with the graffiti theme. The headings for each page using a red circle background with white lettering were also inspired by a logo from a popular spray company named Krylon. Each page was meant to feel like a blank wall waiting to be sprayed on.
