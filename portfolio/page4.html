Toxic
Tears

Toxic Tears was a fictional hot sauce brand created in my Logo and Identity Design course. The goal of the logo was to bring feelings of intensity and danger through the name and symbols on the logo. The nuclear symbol was a main inspiration for the color combination and layout of the peppers around the interior of the logo. The nuclear symbol with the peppers helps further push into the explosive nature that the hot sauce will bring.
