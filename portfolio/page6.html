Typography
Poster

I created this poster in my Typography class. History’s greatest typographers were meant to be highlighted in this work. With the title and headings, I used a more rounded sans-serif font. The design uses grid layout to help organize the section into clear historical sections. The design is inspired by modernist and Bauhaus aesthetics with the use of primary colors, geometrical shapes, and asymmetrical balance. The left side of the piece is a more abstract form that is both decorative and a visual center point for guiding the viewer.
