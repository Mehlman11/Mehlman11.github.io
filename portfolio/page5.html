Eunoia

Eunoia was a fictional fitness and sleep app created in my Digital Design course. The app was a project created by me and my partner Carter. The name was derived from a Greek word meaning ‘well-being”, which translated well with the purpose of our app to create a better lifestyle for all people. The logo being a flower was meant to show how our health needs constant work to thrive and be more efficient, like a flower needing constant water and sunlight to prosper as well. The color scheme being orange, red, and yellow also brings in this same idea that a fire needs constant maintenance to keep burning. The app featured user assistance, activity tracking, progress graphs for workouts, and sleep data visualization. Gradients of red and orange are used throughout the app to bring feelings of warmth and positivity to keep users engaged while being in the app.
